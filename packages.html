Interactive Three-course Dinner Cooking Classes

Bring your friends or family together for a lively and interactive culinary adventure in your own home. Our private cooking classes are designed to walk groups of 8 to 14 through the steps and skills required to create and present a three or four course meal. Cooking class menus are focused on popular regional cuisines. Cooking class participants work in pairs preparing each menu item, gaining skills and confidence, and of course having fun! Afterwards, the group enjoys the fruits of their labour and sits down to share a meal, a glass of wine or cocktails and some laughs. If your group is looking for more of a challenge or to add some excitement to the evening, consider the Master Chef or Black Box challenge.
